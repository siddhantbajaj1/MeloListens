About MELOLISTENS.COM


There have been millions of artists in the world, and zillions of songs. To keep track of all of them would be next to impossible. We are here to do that next-to-impossible task. At MeloListens, you can find records of information on a large number of songs, albums, or artists, each having a page of their own. You can check what Genre a particular singer sings in, or what album does the latest song on your i-pod belongs to. Once you have added enough songs to your library, we will also give you recommendations based on your favorite songs' choice. On most of the song pages, you can listen to a sample of the song too.MeloListens is like a hybrid of Goodreads and MySpace.

Adding to database

MeloListens relies on information supplied by it's users to keep it's database in the state-of-the-art. For this reason, anyone who has a account on MeloListens can add a song here. Once a moderator verifies it, the song is added to our database.

Groups

You can interact with other MeloListeners by groups and discussions.

Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us here.



To add a new album or artist, just add a new song from/by that album/artist here. We'll automatically add the corresponding album/artist.
