"Amazing" is a power ballad performed by the American hard rock band Aerosmith. Don Henley lends his vocals shadowing Steven Tyler in parts of this song. It was written by the lead singer Steven Tyler and a longtime band friend and collaborator Richie Supa. It peaked at #24 on the Billboard Hot 100, #3 on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, and #9 on the Top 40 Mainstream chart,[1] and #57 in the UK.
